    $在\Delta x取极限时,微分dy等于线性主部f’(x_0)dx=\frac{dy(x_0)}{dx}dx；当\Delta x=1时，则差分\Delta y[x]=y[x+1]-y[x]$；
    二阶差分$\Delta^2 y[x]=\Delta (\Delta y[x])=\Delta(y[x+1]-y[x])=y[x+2]-2y[x+1]+y[x]$
    三阶差分$\Delta^3 y[x]=\Delta(\Delta^2 y[x])$

    差分算子性质：
    1）线性：$\Delta(au_{t}+bv_{t})=a\Delta u_{t}+b\Delta v_{t}$
    2)$乘法性质：\Delta(u_{t}·v_{t})=u_{t+1}·v_{t+1}-u_{t}·v_{t}
    =v_{t}·\Delta u_{t}+\color{red}{u_{t+1}}·\Delta v_{t}=u_{t}·\Delta v_{t}+\color{red}{v_{t+1}}·\Delta u_{t}$
    3)除法性质：$\Delta(\frac{u_{t}}{v_{t}})=\frac{u_{t+1}}{v_{t+1}}-\frac{u_{t}}{v_{t}}=\frac{v_{t}\Delta u_{t}-u_{t}\Delta v_{t}}{v_{t+1}v_{t}}$

    1 一阶常系数线性差分方程

    $y[x+1] - ay[x] = f(x)$

    1.1 齐次$f(x)=0$

    设$y[x]=Ca^{ x}$;代入原方程；$Ca^{x+1}-Ca^{ x+1}=0$；其通解为秩=1的线性无关组

    1.2 非齐次$f(x)≠0$

    设特解$y_1[x],y_2[x]$；则$y_1[x+1] - ay_1[x] -(y_2[x+1]-ay_2[x])= f(x) - f(x)$⇒$y_1[x+1]-y_2[x+1]-a(y_1[x]-y_2[x])=0$⇒$y_1[x]-y_2[x]=Ca^x$

    则通解$y[x]=y^*[x]+Ca^x$
    特解由f(x)的形式决定（注意维持线性无关性）：
    $当f(x)=C·p^{t}时设特解y^{}=\begin{cases} k·p^{t}(p\ne a)\ kt·p^{t}(p=a)\ \end{cases}$$当f(x)=p_{0}+p_{1}t+…+p_{m}t^{m}时,$$设特解为y^{}=\begin{cases} P_{0}+P_{1}t+…+P_{m}t^{m}(a\ne 1)\ t·(P_{0}+P_{1}t+…+P_{m}t^{m})(a=1)\ \end{cases}$$当f(t)=m^{t}(p_{1}cos\beta t+p_{2}sin\beta t)时,$$设特解为y^{*}=\begin{cases}m^{t}(C_{1}cos\beta t+C_{2}sin\beta t) \quad m(cos\beta+sin\beta i)不是特征根时\ tm^{t}(C_{1}cos\beta t+C_{2}sin\beta t) \quad m(cos\beta+sin\beta i)是特征方程的根时\ \end{cases}$

    2 二阶常系数线性差分方程

    $y[x+2]+ay[x+1]+by[x]=f(x)$

    2.1 齐次$f(x)=0$

    设$特解y^*[x]=\lambda^x$,带入原式：$\lambda^2+a\lambda+b=0$;
    $其通解为秩2的线性无关组合；通解为\begin{cases} y=C_{1}\lambda^{t}{1}+C{2}\lambda^{t}{2},\lambda{1}与\lambda_{2}是两个不相等的实根\ y=(C_{1}+C_{2}t)\lambda_{1}^{t},\lambda_{1}与\lambda_{2}是两个相等的实根\ y=(C_{1}cos\theta t+C_{2}sin\theta t)r^{t},\lambda_{1,2}=\alpha\pm\beta i\ 其中tan\theta=\frac{\beta}{\alpha}(0<\theta<\pi),r=\sqrt{\alpha^{2}+\beta^{2}} \end{cases}$

    2.2 非齐次$f(x)≠0$

    设特解$y_1[x],y_2[x]$：$y_1[x+2]+ay_1[x+1]+by_1[x]-(y_2[x+2]+ay_2[x+1]+by_2[x])=f(x)-f(x)$⇒
    $y_1[x+2]-y_2[x+2]+a(y_1[x+1]-y_2[x+1])+b(y_1[x]-y_2[x])=0$⇒
    通解$y[x]=y_0[x]+y^*[x]$

    $当f(x)=C·p^{t}时设特解y^{}=\begin{cases} k·p^{t}(p不是特征根)\ kt·p^{t}(p是特征方程的单根)\ kt^{2}·p^{t}(p是特征方程的重根)\ \end{cases}$$当f(x)=p_{0}+p_{1}t+…+p_{m}t^{m}时,$$设特解y^{}=\begin{cases} P_{0}+P_{1}t+…+P_{m}t^{m}(1+a+b\ne 0)\ t·(P_{0}+P_{1}t+…+P_{m}t^{m})(1+a+b=0且a\ne-2)\ t^{2}·(P_{0}+P_{1}t+…+P_{m}t^{m})(a=-2且b=1)\ \end{cases}$$当f(t)=m^{t}(p_{1}cos\beta t+p_{2}sin\beta t)时,$$设特解为y^{*}=\begin{cases}m^{t}(C_{1}cos\beta t+C_{2}sin\beta t) \quad m(cos\beta+sin\beta i)不是特征根时\ tm^{t}(C_{1}cos\beta t+C_{2}sin\beta t) \quad m(cos\beta+sin\beta i)是特征方程的单根时\ t^{2}m^{t}(C_{1}cos\beta t+C_{2}sin\beta t)\quad m(cos\beta+sin\beta i)是特征方程的重根时\ \end{cases}$
